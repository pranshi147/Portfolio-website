My Hobbies

  • Sports

  • I’ve always been someone who enjoys almost every sport—there’s just something exciting about trying new games and staying active. But the ones I connect with the most are badminton and football. Badminton keeps me sharp and fast on my feet, while football gives me the thrill of teamwork and high-energy play. No matter what I try, these two sports will always be my favourites to play.

  • Reading

  • I enjoy reading a lot because it lets me step away from the real world and dive into new ideas, stories, and perspectives. I read different kinds of books, but fictional fantasy and motivational books are my favourites. Fantasy takes me into magical worlds full of adventure and imagination, while motivational books inspire me to grow, stay focused, and keep pushing forward.

  • Travelling

  • I love travelling because it lets me explore new places, meet different people, and experience things I’ve never seen before. Every trip feels like a chance to learn something new—whether it’s the culture, the food, or just the vibe of the place.

  • Listening to Music

  • I love listening to music—it’s honestly something I can’t go a day without. I’m really into R&B, and some of my favourite artists are Chase Atlantic, The Neighbourhood, and Azee. Their music just hits in a different way.
